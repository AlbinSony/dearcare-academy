Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our courses and training programs

What are the eligibility criteria for your courses?

Most of our courses require a minimum qualification of SSLC (10th grade). There are no strict age limits, making our programs accessible to anyone passionate about healthcare and nursing.

Do you provide placement assistance after course completion?

Yes, we offer comprehensive placement assistance to all our graduates. We have strong partnerships with leading hospitals, healthcare centers, and clinics across Kerala and beyond, ensuring excellent career opportunities for our students.

What is the duration of the courses and training schedule?

Our courses typically run for 45 days, combining both theoretical and practical training. We offer flexible learning options to accommodate different schedules, with practical sessions conducted at partner healthcare facilities.

How is the practical training conducted?

Practical training takes place in well-equipped healthcare facilities including hospitals, clinics, and specialized care centers. Students receive hands-on experience under expert supervision, ensuring they develop real-world skills.

Are your certifications recognized in the healthcare industry?

Yes, our certifications are widely recognized in the healthcare sector. Our courses are designed in alignment with industry standards and requirements, making our graduates highly valued in the job market.
