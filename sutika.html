Sutika Paricharya Course

Traditional Ayurvedic Postpartum Care

Program Overview

The Sutika Paricharya course focuses on traditional Ayurvedic practices for postpartum care, offering a holistic approach to a mother's physical and emotional recovery after childbirth. Rooted in ancient Ayurvedic wisdom, this course provides essential techniques and therapies designed to support new mothers in their postpartum journey.

Duration: 3-4 months (depending on practical components)

Mode: In-person or Hybrid (Online & In-person)

Level: Beginner to Intermediate

Course Objectives

Comprehensive Understanding

Gain a comprehensive understanding of Ayurveda and its principles

Postpartum Traditions

Learn the importance of postpartum care for new mothers and infants in Ayurvedic traditions

Holistic Support

Acquire knowledge and practical skills to provide holistic postpartum support

Treatment Mastery

Master Ayurvedic treatments, dietary recommendations, and lifestyle practices

Recovery Management

Address common physical and emotional imbalances during postpartum recovery

Mind-Body Wellness

Integrate traditional healing practices for complete mental and physical wellbeing

Course Curriculum

01 Introduction to Ayurveda

  • History, philosophy, and basic principles (Panchamahabhuta & Tridosha)
  • Concept of Prakriti (body constitution) and Vikriti (imbalances)
  • Importance of Ayurveda in women's health and maternity care

02 Ayurvedic Perspective on Postpartum Recovery

  • Understanding the postpartum phase (Sutika Kala) and the first 42 days
  • Physiological and emotional changes in new mothers
  • Importance of rest, nourishment, and rejuvenation

03 Stages of Postpartum Healing & Vata-Balancing

  • Ayurvedic stages of recovery and balancing Vata Dosha
  • Common postpartum complications and Ayurvedic management

04 Postpartum Nutrition & Dietary Guidelines

  • Role of Agni (digestive fire) in recovery
  • Importance of warm, nourishing, and easily digestible foods
  • Traditional postpartum diets and herbal tonics for lactation
  • Ayurvedic meal planning for new mothers

05 Herbal Remedies for Postpartum Care

  • Ayurvedic herbs like Dashamoola, Shatavari, and Ashwagandha
  • Herbal formulations for digestion, immunity, and strength
  • Safe remedies for fatigue, constipation, and low milk supply

06 Ayurvedic Therapies for Healing

  • Abhyanga (oil massage) and Swedana (herbal steam therapy)
  • Ubtan (herbal scrub), Yoni Pichu, and Vasti treatments
  • Udvartana (dry massage) for post-delivery weight management

07 Emotional & Mental Well-being

  • Self-care, mindfulness, and managing postpartum stress/anxiety
  • Ayurvedic approach to postpartum depression
  • Pranayama, yoga asanas, and meditation for recovery

08 Ayurvedic Newborn Care

  • Samskaras and Swasthya Raksha (neonatal care)
  • Ayurvedic baby massage and sleep techniques
  • Safe herbal remedies for common infant ailments
  • Guidelines for breastfeeding and infant nutrition

09 Special Ayurvedic Practices

  • Role of Dhoopana (medicinal fumigation) in baby care
  • Application of Ayurvedic lepas for pain relief
  • Use of Ayurvedic oils and ghees for postpartum recovery
